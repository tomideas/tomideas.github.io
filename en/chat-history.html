Chat History

Open History

Use the history button at the top of the sidebar to slide out the panel where you can view and manage past conversations.

Features

  • Browse Conversations: View all saved conversations sorted by time.
  • Select & Batch Operations: Supports select-all and batch deletion.
  • Export: Export conversation content for backup.
  • Delete: Delete individual or selected conversations.

Storage Mechanism

Conversation data is stored in chrome.storage.local and is not synced to other devices via Chrome. The system automatically cleans up old conversations to save space.

ℹ️ Empty Conversations Empty conversations are not saved.
