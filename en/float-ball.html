Float Ball

A floating shortcut button on the webpage that lets you quickly open the sidebar without going through the extension menu.

Enable Float Ball

Go to Settings → Sidebar → Show Float Ball to enable this feature.

How to Use

  • Click: Opens the sidebar chat.
  • Drag: Move the float ball to a different position. The position is remembered and will persist the next time you visit the same site.
💡 Tip You can drag the float ball to the edge of the screen where it's less obtrusive, or disable it entirely and use a custom shortcut to open the sidebar instead.
